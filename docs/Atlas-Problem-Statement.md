# Atlas Problem Statement

Version: 0.1

---

## The Problem

China's capital market does not lack information.

It lacks trusted cognition.

Every trading day, ordinary investors are surrounded by:

- company announcements
- news
- short videos
- social media
- opinions
- rumors

The problem is not the amount of information.

The problem is that investors cannot determine:

- What really matters?
- What affects me?
- What should I ignore?

This creates information anxiety.

---

## Our Observation

Ordinary investors repeatedly open stock applications.

Not because they need more information.

But because they seek certainty.

Most information only increases anxiety.

Very little information improves understanding.

---

## Atlas Exists Because

Atlas is designed to answer one question:

"What really matters today?"

Instead of delivering more information,

Atlas filters noise,

connects events,

tracks outcomes,

and preserves market memory.

---

## What Atlas Will Never Become

Atlas will never become:

- a stock recommendation system
- an AI prediction tool
- a trading platform
- an opinion media

Atlas exists to organize reality.

Not to manufacture certainty.

---

## Product Mission

Help ordinary investors spend less time chasing information,

and more time understanding reality.
