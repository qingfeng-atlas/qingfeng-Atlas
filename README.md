# Project Atlas

> We don't predict the market.
>
> We help investors understand it.

---

# Mission

Project Atlas is building a Capital Market Event Graph for ordinary investors.

The market does not lack information.

The market lacks structured, verifiable and continuously evolving knowledge.

Atlas exists to transform fragmented information into long-term investment cognition.

---

# Vision

To become the most trusted capital market knowledge infrastructure for ordinary investors.

Not by giving investment advice.

But by recording every important event, connecting every relationship, and preserving every historical outcome.

---

# What Atlas Is

Atlas is NOT:

- A stock recommendation system
- An AI stock picker
- A trading platform
- A news portal

Atlas IS:

- A Capital Market Event Database
- A Capital Market Memory Engine
- A Capital Market Knowledge Graph
- A Fact Engine

---

# Our Principles

## 1. Facts First

Facts before opinions.

Evidence before conclusions.

---

## 2. Event First

Markets are driven by events.

Not by headlines.

---

## 3. History Matters

Every event deserves to be remembered.

Every outcome deserves to be verified.

---

## 4. Long-term Thinking

We are building a database that should still be valuable ten years from now.

---

# What We Believe

Ordinary investors do not need more information.

They need fewer distractions.

Atlas does not help users chase every piece of news.

Atlas helps users focus on the few events that truly matter.
