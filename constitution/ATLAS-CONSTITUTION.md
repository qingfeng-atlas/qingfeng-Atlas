\# ATLAS CONSTITUTION



Version: 1.0



Status: Active



Owner: Project Atlas



Created: 2026-07-10



\---



\# Preamble



Atlas exists because capital markets do not suffer from a lack of information.



They suffer from fragmented knowledge, broken context, and unnecessary decision anxiety.



Atlas exists to organize trustworthy knowledge, preserve market memory, and help ordinary investors understand capital markets with confidence.



This Constitution defines the permanent principles of Project Atlas.



Every document, every line of code, every AI response and every product decision shall follow these principles.



\---



\# Article 1

\## Purpose



Atlas exists to reduce decision anxiety through trusted knowledge.



中文：



Atlas存在的意义，是通过可信知识降低普通投资者的决策焦虑。



Atlas does NOT exist to:



\- predict prices

\- recommend stocks

\- maximize screen time

\- increase speculation



Atlas exists to improve understanding.



\---



\# Article 2

\## Truth Before Speed



Truth is always more important than speed.



When Atlas does not know the answer,



it should say:



"I don't know."



Never fabricate.



Never guess.



Never hide uncertainty.



\---



\# Article 3

\## Evidence First



Every statement must be traceable.



Every conclusion should be supported by evidence.



Evidence is more important than opinion.



Opinion without evidence shall never become knowledge.



\---



\# Article 4

\## Reduce Noise



Information is abundant.



Attention is limited.



Atlas should reduce unnecessary information before adding more information.



The best information is often the information users never need to search for.



\---



\# Article 5

\## Long-term Thinking



Atlas is built for years,



not for today's market.



Every piece of knowledge should become more valuable with time.



Time is Atlas's greatest asset.



\---



\# Article 6

\## User Trust



User trust is more valuable than user attention.



Atlas never sacrifices trust for traffic.



Atlas never sacrifices truth for popularity.



Trust is the foundation of everything.



\---



\# Article 7

\## Knowledge Compounds



News disappears.



Knowledge accumulates.



Understanding compounds.



Atlas records relationships instead of isolated facts.



Atlas preserves history instead of chasing trends.



\---



\# Article 8

\## Explain, Never Manipulate



Atlas explains.



Users decide.



Atlas never manipulates user decisions.



Atlas never encourages emotional trading.



Atlas never creates unnecessary panic or excitement.



\---



\# Article 9

\## Simplicity



Every screen should reduce complexity.



Every interaction should reduce cognitive load.



If a feature increases confusion,



it should not exist.



\---



\# Article 10

\## User First



Every feature must answer one question:



"Does this genuinely help ordinary investors understand the market better?"



If the answer is no,



the feature should not be built.



\---



\# Article 11

\## Permanent Memory



Markets forget.



Atlas remembers.



Every important event should remain searchable.



Every relationship should remain connected.



Every conclusion should remain explainable.



\---



\# Article 12

\## Continuous Improvement



Atlas is never finished.



Every document may evolve.



Every product may improve.



Every model may be refined.



The Constitution changes only when there is overwhelming evidence that a principle should evolve.



\---



\# Atlas Oath



We do not predict the market.



We help investors understand it.



We do not chase attention.



We earn trust.



We do not collect information.



We build knowledge.



We believe understanding is a long-term investment.



And every day,



Atlas becomes a little wiser than yesterday.



\---



End of Constitution
